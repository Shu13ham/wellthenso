Our Process

01

ENQUIRY

Fill up our Contact Form or visit our studio for us to better understand your needs and requirements. We will then get back to you!

02

Requirement Gathering

Where we learn about your organization and requirements. Initial consultation to determine scope of work, user needs and budget. Site visit to measure and record existing conditions.

03

Design

Where we generate ideas and brainstorm solutions. Preliminary design intention and direction, Mood board + Theme board. Preliminary space plan, furniture and finished selections. Present preliminary design, solicit feedback and gain approval.

04

Draft

Where we develop and finalize design. Develop working drawings, plan sets and renderings. Finalize selections of furniture,finishes and materials. Prepare estimated budget and timeline.

05

Execution

Material is procured and vendors are mobilised. Progressive Site visits to ensure design intent is actualized. Maintain project records. Onsite supervision of installation.
