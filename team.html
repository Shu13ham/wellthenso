WELLTHENSO

Our People

WellThenSo was founded with the belief that spaces shape the way we live, think, and feel. Since 2019, the studio has brought together a team of interior designers, furniture specialists, and architects with a shared love for thoughtful design and honest materials.

ach project is approached with care and collaboration balancing aesthetic intention with everyday functionality. Our people listen deeply, design purposefully, and build with quiet confidence. The spaces we craft speak of their inhabitants, their rituals, and the moments in between.
